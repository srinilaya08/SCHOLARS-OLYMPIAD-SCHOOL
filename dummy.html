Client Referrals

Please see the information below to begin the referral process at Redeemed Ministries.

To support women who have been victimized and exploited, we follow specific intake guidelines. Review our qualifications, scope of care, and application steps to help us process referrals quickly and with care.

Who Can Apply

  • Referred by law enforcement or anti-trafficking agency
  • 18+ years old
  • Survivor of sex trafficking
  • U.S. Citizen
  • Biologically female
  • No immediate threats from others
  • Free from drug/alcohol withdrawal (30+ days)
  • Not a danger to self or others
  • Willing to heal and change
  • Within our scope of care

Who Does Not Qualify

  • Pregnant
  • Currently addicted and not in rehab
  • Taking methadone, Suboxone, or narcotics
  • Unable to care for self (non-ambulatory)
  • Has children in her custody (without long-term care arrangements)
  • Serious health or mental conditions
  • Diagnosed with Hepatitis, HIV/AIDS, or TB
  • Has warrants or court needs out-of-state
  • In a mental health crisis (suicidal/homicidal)

Application Process

  1. Fill out the online CLIENT REFERRAL FORM
  2. Phone call with the agency referring the client
  3. Background check completed
  4. Assessments completed
  5. In-person interview with the applicant
  6. Processing may take 1–3 weeks (local clients may stay in emergency housing during this time)

Have Questions?

Email us at: referrals@redeemedtx.org

Client Referrals

Please see the information below to begin the referral process.

Who Can Qualify?

  • Referral from law enforcement or anti-trafficking agency
  • 18 years or older
  • Survivor of sex trafficking
  • U.S. Citizen and biologically female
  • No immediate threat from others
  • Free from substance withdrawal (30 days clean)
  • Not a danger to self or others
  • Willing and ready to heal from trauma
  • Falls within our scope of care

Who May Not Qualify

  • Pregnant or not willing to go through detox
  • On methadone/Suboxone or narcotics
  • Unable to walk or care for self
  • Has custody of children without long-term care
  • Severe medical or mental health diagnosis
  • Diagnosed with Hepatitis, HIV/AIDS, or Tuberculosis
  • Has warrants or legal travel restrictions
  • Experiencing a mental health crisis

Application Process

  1. Submit the Online Client Referral Form
  2. Phone call with the referring agency
  3. Complete a background check
  4. Finish required assessments
  5. Attend in-person interview
  6. Timeline: 1 to 3 weeks (locals may stay in a shelter)

Questions? Reach out to us at referrals@redeemedtx.org

Why Sparrow House Works

Every woman’s journey is unique. Our trauma-informed program provides support, guidance, and space for women to reclaim their lives—one step at a time.

  • 🕊️ Christ-centered or secular curriculum
  • 🎨 Art, music, equine & writing therapy
  • 🏡 Safe home setting with live-in care coaches
  • 💼 Economic empowerment & job readiness
  • 🌿 Fully funded – no cost to participate
  • 📚 Trauma-informed & nationally aligned care
